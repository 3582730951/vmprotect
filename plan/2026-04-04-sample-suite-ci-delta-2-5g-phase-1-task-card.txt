## 目标
- 修掉 macOS validator 对三处 `-isysroot` 的 path-identity 假失败
- 保留 Delta-2.5f / Delta-2.5e 的既有 strict gate

## 机械步骤
1. 保留 macOS validator 当前 `required` 列表，不增删 key。
2. 保留现有：
   - `if set(kv) != set(required): raise SystemExit("[FAIL] key set mismatch")`
3. 在 `command_payload_tokens` 后新增：
   - `extract_flag_value_paths(command_text: str, flag: str) -> list[Path]`
   - `assert_flag_path_matches_expected(command_text: str, command_key: str, flag: str, expected_path: Path, fail_msg: str) -> None`
4. `extract_flag_value_paths(...)` 固定实现：
   - 先 `tokens = command_payload_tokens(command_text)`
   - 只接受：
     - `-isysroot <path>`
     - `-isysroot=<path>`
   - 不接受其它拼写
   - 匹配值直接转成 `Path`
5. `assert_flag_path_matches_expected(...)` 固定顺序：
   - 候选数不等于 1 -> `raise SystemExit(fail_msg)`
   - 不是绝对路径 -> `raise SystemExit(fail_msg)`
   - 不存在 -> `raise SystemExit(fail_msg)`
   - 先试 `candidate.samefile(expected_path)`
   - 仅当抛 `OSError` 或 `FileNotFoundError` 时，再试 `candidate.resolve(strict=False) == expected_path.resolve(strict=False)`
   - 禁止 `except Exception`
   - 仍不相等 -> `raise SystemExit(fail_msg)`
6. 删除当前三条旧逻辑：
   - `if "-isysroot" not in ios_helper_text or str(Path(kv["ios_isysroot"]).resolve()) not in ios_helper_text`
   - `if "-isysroot" not in protected_compile_text or str(ios_sdk) not in protected_compile_text`
   - `if "-isysroot" not in command_text or str(ios_sdk) not in command_text`
7. 用三次函数调用替换：
   - `assert_flag_path_matches_expected(ios_helper_text, "ios_user_helper_compile_command", "-isysroot", Path(kv["ios_isysroot"]), "[FAIL] ios_user_helper_compile_command missing isysroot")`
   - `assert_flag_path_matches_expected(protected_compile_text, "ios_protected_compile_command", "-isysroot", Path(kv["ios_isysroot"]), "[FAIL] ios_protected_compile_command missing isysroot")`
   - `assert_flag_path_matches_expected(command_text, "ios_link_command", "-isysroot", Path(kv["ios_isysroot"]), "[FAIL] ios_link_command missing isysroot")`
8. 保留：
   - `if Path(kv["ios_isysroot"]).resolve() != ios_sdk: raise ...`
   - `assert_cmake_define_matches_expected(...)`
   - `plugin_build_log` / `plugin_link_command` 既有 gate
9. 不改 Linux / Windows / Android validator
10. 不改 `build_macos_ios.sh`
